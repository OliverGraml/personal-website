Introduction

After my military career, I went back to school to get my High School Diploma from Bundeswehrfachschule. Then I did my bachelor's degree at Munich School of Philosophy and currently I am doing my master's at the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität Munich. Next to my studies, I'am the Managing Director of Montessori Unterschleißheim e.V., a non-profit association that runs a private school with 30 employees. My tasks there include the general management, setting the strategic course of the society, planning a new school building and currently the digital change management in school and society.
